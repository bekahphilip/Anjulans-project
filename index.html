"We're Not Your Typical Florist"


Artistry, passion and knowledge are all elements vital to wedding and special event floral design. Striking colors, rich textures, lingering scents and inspired design combine to make wedding and special event flowers by Anjulans' extraordinary.
As a florist based out of Rehoboth, MA, Anjulans' commitment to amazing floral design and personal service is unsurpassed. Anjulans' is a florist known for using the highest quality flowers in creative and memorable floral arrangements.
Anjulans' services include wedding flower décor, special event décor rentals , event wireless uplighting and unique pinspot downlighting effects over your centerpieces
